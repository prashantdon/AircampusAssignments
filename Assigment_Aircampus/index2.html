FOOD IS LOVE!!

What makes you love a food?
IT TASTES GOOD. Despite all of the other great things food brings to the table, people eat food
because of the way it tastes. Whether it's the sweetness of the glaze on a donut or the spicy flavor
of wasabi on a sushi roll, eating and enjoying good tasting food is what a food lovers life is all about.
